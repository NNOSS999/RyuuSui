「竜粋堂」

「竜粋堂」is headquartered in Japan and focuses on producing high-quality food and beverages, covering a wide range of product categories. We not only inherit the classic recipes of Mr. Kenichi Furukawa but also continuously innovate to meet the modern consumers' pursuit of deliciousness, convenience, and health.

The R&D team at「竜粋堂」consists of a group of experienced professional chefs who possess years of practical experience and deep culinary expertise in the food and beverage industry. This enables「竜粋堂」to strive for perfection in every product's development process, focusing on flavor, texture, and formulation to recreate the deliciousness of freshly made dishes. Our goal is for every customer to experience the refinement and uniqueness of our cuisine through our products, providing consumers with a touching dining experience. At the same time, we always prioritize food safety.「竜粋堂」strictly adheres to international food safety standards, ensuring that every step, from ingredient selection to production and processing, is rigorously controlled to guarantee the quality and safety of each product. We comprehensively manage our supply chain and utilize the most advanced technology and facilities to ensure consumers can enjoy every bite with peace of mind.

Looking to the world

Whether through our online store or offline retail, we are dedicated to providing global consumers with fast and convenient purchasing channels, ensuring that high-quality products are within reach. We firmly believe that food knows no borders. 「竜粋堂」will continue to promote the spread and development of Chinese and Japanese culinary cultures worldwide with our excellent product quality and diverse product range. No matter where you are, you can enjoy the most authentic and exquisite Japanese cuisine through「竜粋堂」.

Our History

August 2019

At the age of 37, Kenichi Furukawa opened his own ramen shop,「竜粋堂」

October 2019

Kenichi Furukawa's condition deteriorated after contracting the novel coronavirus, and he passed away at the age of 37.

December 2024

Kenichi Furukawa's friends inherited his vision and established Ryuusui Co., Ltd.

Charitable Work

We firmly believe that a company's success is not only measured by its business achievements but also by its contributions to society. Therefore, we actively participate in various charitable activities, committed to helping those in need and giving back to the community.

Each year, we donate food and funds to impoverished areas around the world, helping to improve living conditions and bringing warmth and hope to those in difficult circumstances. We also pay special attention to children's health issues by donating to medical funds to help children who cannot afford treatment due to financial constraints receive necessary medical assistance, allowing them to grow up healthy and have a brighter future.

「竜粋堂」believes that through our efforts, we can bring about change for more people. We are committed not only to providing the world with delicious food but also to conveying love and care through charitable work, creating a brighter future for impoverished families and children around the globe.
